The five variables
Contract drift
The gap between the story they're selling and what the audience actually sees. Scored 1 to 10.
Root cause
Shame or betrayal. The audience felt foolish or they felt sold out. Different breaks. Different endings.
Contract depth
How deep the audience went in. Surface investors exit quietly. True believers become the loudest critics on the way out.
Collapse stage
Zero to five. Drift to inversion. Stage two is the last point where any of this is still fixable.
Immunity load
How much runway is left. When it runs out, the next mistake isn't a mistake. It's the verdict.
