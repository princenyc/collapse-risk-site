A five-variable diagnostic for artist legitimacy collapse. Not a prediction. A probability map. Built on the Chartreuse Explosives framework.

The five variables
Contract drift
How far they've fallen from who they claimed to be. Scored 1 to 10.
Root cause
Did the audience feel foolish or betrayed. Two different breaks. Two different endings.
Contract depth
How much of the audience's identity is wrapped up in this person. Determines how loud the exit gets.
Collapse stage
Five stages from drift to inversion. The difference between them is fixable versus permanent.
Immunity load
How many mistakes they have left before the math changes permanently.

Type any artist, politician, athlete, or public figure. The instrument runs the full five-variable analysis and tells you exactly where they stand.
