Questions
What's the difference between Signal and paid reports?
Signal runs on training data through mid-2025. No web search. It knows the shape of someone's career but not what happened last month. Intel and Archive run live web search on every report. Current events. Named sources. Specific receipts. The analysis is different in kind, not just in depth.
Do Archive reports really never expire?
Never. Buy 10 reports today, use the last one two years from now. No monthly pressure. No wasted credits. The balance stays until you use it.
Do I need an account for Intel?
No. $5, one report, no account. Pay and run. The report is yours to export and share immediately.
What does the prescription actually tell me?
Every report ends with a specific prescription — what the subject should do next based on their exact collapse stage and root cause. Not generic advice. Named moves. You'll know what the right play is before they do.
Does this work for politicians, athletes, and brands — not just musicians?
Yes. The framework was built for musicians but the mechanism is identical across any public figure whose position depends on an audience choosing to believe in them. Politicians, athletes, executives, brands — the five variables scale to all of them.
What if I need more than 10 reports?
Buy another Archive bundle. They stack. Each purchase adds 10 reports to your balance.
