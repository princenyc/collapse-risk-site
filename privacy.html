Effective June 19, 2026 · Applies to collapse.chartreuseexplosives.com
What we collect

Free reports. Your email address. Your IP address. The subject name you run. The report we generate. Whether you checked the updates box. The time you ran it.

Paid reports. Stripe collects your email and payment details when you check out. We never see your card number. Stripe hands us your email so we can issue and email you a license key.

License key activation. The key itself. Nothing else. Activating a key doesn't require an account or any new personal data.

Why we collect it

Email and IP enforce the one free report limit. Without them, the free tier isn't free, it's unlimited, and the math behind this product breaks.

The subject name goes to the engine that writes your report. Nothing about you personally goes with it beyond that name.

If you checked the updates box, we use your email to send occasional updates about Chartreuse Explosives. If you didn't check it, we don't.

Who else sees it
  • Stripe processes every payment. We don't touch your card details at any point.
  • Resend delivers license key emails and any update emails you opted into.
  • Anthropic processes the subject name and runs the live search behind paid reports.
  • Cloudflare hosts the site and stores the records described on this page.

Each of those companies runs under its own privacy policy, not ours. We don't sell your data. Not to advertisers, not to data brokers, not to anyone. That's not a marketing line, it's just not part of how this business works.

How long we keep it
  • Email and IP tied to your free report limit: kept indefinitely, because the limit is one report per lifetime, not one per year.
  • Your email and the subject you searched (the lead record): kept up to two years, then deleted automatically.
  • Paid report pages at permanent links: kept one year, then the link expires.
  • License keys: kept one year.
Cookies and tracking

No advertising trackers run on this site. No third-party analytics pixels. The only data leaving your browser is what you type in and what your browser sends automatically, like your IP address.

Your rights

You can ask what we have on you. You can ask us to delete it. You can opt out of updates at any time. Email us to do any of these and we'll act on it.

If you're in the EU or UK, you have rights under GDPR including access, correction, deletion, and objection to processing. If you're a California resident, you have rights under the CCPA including the right to know what's collected and the right to delete it. The mechanism is the same either way: email us.

Children

This site isn't directed at anyone under 16. We don't knowingly collect data from minors. If we learn that we have, we'll delete it.

Changes to this policy

We'll update this page when what we actually do changes. The date at the top tells you when that last happened. We won't quietly expand what we collect without updating it here first.

Contact

Questions, deletion requests, or anything else covered here: privacy@chartreuseexplosives.com
